How we performed at 20 conferences in Dubai

04 Nov 2022

The month of October 2022 was the busiest and most productive for the Rock’n’Block team, we participated in 6 exhibitions, presented 2 booths, one of them in the partnership, and visited more than 10 side events in Dubai, UAE.

It has become an annual tradition for Rock’n’Block to devote its time to offline events in October, and many of the conferences are not the first time we've come to them. This is a unique opportunity to meet with our clients and colleagues in the era of digital business processes. We went to all the conferences as a small team of business developers who are responsible for building strong relationships with our partners.

Statistics indicate that Dubai is now preparing to become the global center of the promising crypto market. Major cryptocurrency exchanges, estimated to be worth billions of dollars, are opening an office in Dubai.

The main topics of all Blockchain events are Web 3, NFT, Metaverse, De-Fi, cryptocurrency regulation, cryptocurrency investing and trading, and blockchain technology implementation in business and government. In the latter, we are specialists and update expertise.

Let's begin to dive into our event journey in chronological order.

Blockchain Economy Summit

Blockchain Economy Summit, the world's largest blockchain, and crypto conference series took place on October 4-6, 2022 in Dubai, UAE. The excitement of this exclusive summit is no stranger to its fans.

Rock’n’Block acted as a silver sponsor of the event this time. We would like to take this opportunity to thank the organizers of this event for promptly resolving issues and providing first-class service in the installation of a business stand.

Thanks to our bright stand that stands out from the crowd, we were able to attract dozens of new potential customers and partners for the further development of ecosystems using blockchain technology.

To crown on it all an unexpected invitation to the stage and an award in the category of top blockchain development companies. We are pleased with this recognition, our team strives to match the quality of our services.

Crypto Expo Dubai

On October 5-6, 2022, the annual Crypto Expo Dubai 2022 conference was held in Dubai. This is the largest event in Asia, attended by crypto industry leaders. The purpose of the conference was to explore business opportunities and share experiences.

This year the conference was attended by a delegation from Rock'n'Block. Presentations were made by representatives of OKX, Coinhako, Amber Group, The BitcoinMan, Bitmart, Bybit, MarinaChain, and Huobi Global. The Rock'n'Block delegation visited the stands of business partners and held talks on expanding cooperation in Dubai.

World Metaverse Show 

More than 500 industry experts and influencers foreshadowed the dynamics of the brand-customer relationship at the World Metaverse Show in Dubai on October 5-6, 2022.

Organized by the World Blockchain Summit team, the inaugural World Metaverse Show, the largest global series of Blockchain, crypto, and Web 3 events, brought together experts from digital technology developers, entrepreneurs, investors, governments, enterprises, and technology providers. , 3D, VFX, XR and Web3.

The Dubai Metaworld Strategy, led by His Highness Sheikh Hamdan bin Mohammed Al Maktoum, Crown Prince of Dubai, is designed to shape the future of the Metaworld, aiming to make Dubai one of the world's top 10 meta world economies, and a global destination for the Metaverse. 

Future Blockchain Summit and Gitex

The Future Blockchain Summit is the world's largest and most anticipated blockchain forum in the region.  Aimed at unlocking the enormous potential of disruptive technologies, the event is designed to accelerate the global dialogue and attract some of the biggest names in the industry. We also attended this summit in 2021, and read how it was here.

GITEX GLOBAL 2022 takes over Dubai, accelerating the global digital economy. Generally speaking, GITEX is the world's largest and most inclusive technology and startup event with over 100,000 participants and 5,000 exhibitors.  We were lucky enough to have a booth in the startup section together with the MyWish.io project as part of a longstanding and productive collaboration.

MyWish.io is a pioneering platform for creating smart contracts without any coding skills. Automated solutions for any blockchain have been running since 2017.

World Blockchain Summit  

October 17-18, 2022. The event occurred in Dubai because of the local government's favorable stance on crypto. The summit brought together well-known blockchain experts and crypto-enthusiasts to discuss Web 3.0 ecosystems, NFT, and the meta world, as well as the impact of crypto on other areas of society.

The program included:

  • Presentations by leading experts in crypto.

  • A platform for presenting your projects and finding business partners.

  • A drawing for a $1 million prize fund.

This summit was the final big event. but face-to-face meetings and consulting with clients in person in our office do not end to this day.

All the events turned out to be quite useful, and from each, we took away a bunch of useful contacts, from the press to investors. The most stunning conference was, of course, Blockchain Economy because of the unscheduled awards - but all the events were equal in the number of useful contacts.

Our team of blockchain astronauts also attended dozens of side events, private parties, and networking days and nights. The set goals were exceeded, managers were loaded with orders, and our developers had ideas on how to implement all your requests in blockchain development.
