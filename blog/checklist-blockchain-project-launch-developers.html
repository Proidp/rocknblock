Checklist for Blockchain Project Launch. By DEV

05 Aug 2022

Blockchain technology is slowly but surely conquering cyberspace. The Web 3.0 Internet is unfolding in front of our eyes, and it will be very different from what we have been used to seeing for the past 20 years. One of the key features of Web 3.0 is the widespread use of blockchain technology.

  • Team gathering

  • MVP version description

  • Business and financial plan

  • Find and work with advisors

  • Legal issues

  • Launch of a token on launchpool

  • Exchange listing

The wider audience already recognizes that blockchain can be used to create cryptocurrencies, but as Ethereum gained in popularity, it became clear that blockchain is also about smart contracts, decentralized applications, and NFT. Before you start your blockchain project, you need to decide precisely what you want to create.

Blockchain Apps types


If you have decided to implement a blockchain-based application, the next step is to decide on the type of application. Here are some promising ideas:


  • A mobile crypto wallet

  • A launchpad for startups to collect investments.

  • A centralized crypto exchange.

  • Decentralized crypto exchange.

  • NFT marketplace.

  • Crypto-store.

  • Telegram bot for crypto.

  • Browser extension.

  • Play2Earn or Move-to-Earn game using NFT.

  • Decentralized social network.

  • Decentralized streaming platform.


And much, much more. The options are many, but any of them will require an experienced development team.

Team of experts

To create a full-fledged blockchain project, you will need a variety of developers. Front-end developers, Back-end developers. For example, if you work with Ethereum blockchain, you will need specialists in Solidity, if you are going to write a blockchain from scratch specialists in C, C++, C#, Java, and Python will be useful. It is highly recommended that you hire an experienced blockchain development company to successfully turn your idea into a business.

Development process


Once you have decided on the team, it is equally important to decide on the internal architecture of the project. How and what it will work on and what it will be composed of.

blockchain infrastructure: choose wisely and for the long term

The main blockchain platforms are Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, and Solana / Polygon. All have their pros and cons. There is an option to apply your own solution, such as Layer 2 on Ethereum. Some projects opt for two platforms at once: Clash of Coins uses a bundle of Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain.


wallet: prioritize the top choices

Tens of millions of users choose Metamask, Coinbase Wallet, and Trust Wallet with a user-friendly UX/UI. Another wallet worth considering is magic.link.


unified system of technical solutions

To establish strong links between wallet, blockchain, the core gameplay, and the server side, you need to choose a reliable platform for WEB3 development. 


additional services integration

a service for exchanging fiat money into cryptocurrencies

Simplex, Moonpay, and Transak are popular fiat money exchange gateways that provide in-game cryptocurrency purchases for fiat currency.


Depending on the complexity of the chosen project idea, the development process can take a long time, from months to a year if it is a complex ecosystem on a native blockchain network.  To assess the feasibility and functionality of a project, at least an MVP (Minimal Viable Product) should be developed.
 

Working with the MVP

Product: decide on uniqueness and promotion strategy

Networking: build loyal communities

Token launch: gather an audience of a few thousand active users

Security: Identify specialists to audit the security of the project and test the vulnerability


Marketing Production

Creating a project is not enough, you need to market it. The battle for customers is particularly fierce these days because the blockchain industry is growing rapidly in popularity, which means you'll have plenty of competition. You'll need constant social media, blogging, and search engine optimization to attract customers and investment - without all of that, you can't become recognized in the marketplace. Efficient Blockchain Marketing.

Designer and Brand identity

Creating websites, logos, illustrations, graphical interfaces, and presentation documents will all require people who are capable of working with graphics. You can also hire a blockchain development company with experience in such matters.

Management and Project direction

Finally, you will need someone who can coordinate all parts amongst themselves. Being an executive for a blockchain project is not easy, because it requires competence in development, design, marketing, and other related areas, so such specialists are precious and rare.


The development of a blockchain application requires preparatory work with tools and a clear policy on the positioning of the product. Consistent steps and the right decisions in strategic planning will make a blockchain project a success.
